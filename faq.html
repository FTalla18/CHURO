Frequently Asked Questions

What is CHURO?

CHURO is a family-operated car rental business based in Sarasota, Florida, focused on providing personalized, honest, and hassle-free car rentals.

How did CHURO start?

CHURO was born from a desire to offer reliable car rentals without middlemen or surprises. With over 480 trips on Turo and a near-perfect 4.9-star rating, we decided to bring our service directly to our customers.
