About Us

CHURO was born from a simple belief: car rentals should feel personal, honest, and hassle-free.

After completing over 480 trips on Turo with a near-perfect 4.9-star rating, we knew it was time to bring our service directly to you—no middlemen, no surprises. Based in Sarasota and family-operated, we’ve built our business on trust, repeat guests, and a shared love for the open road.

But we’re more than just a car rental company—we’re curators of unforgettable experiences. From spacious 7-seater SUVs to sleek sedans, our fleet is carefully chosen and meticulously maintained to meet your needs. Whether you're in town for the beaches, a family event, or a solo getaway, you’ll find a ride that feels like your own.

At CHURO, every trip is backed by:

  • Clean, reliable Kia Sorento and Optima vehicles
  • Personalized service and local hospitality
  • A commitment to comfort, convenience, and joy

Let us handle the details while you focus on the journey. We're here to earn your business—not just once, but every time you come to town.
